About Us

We are a small bicycle shop located in Oakland's Lake Merrit neighborhood. Cycle Sports has been bringing high quality bicycles to Oakland and the Bay Area since 1991 and we don't expect to stop any time soon. Our dedication to quality and unfailing goal of not cutting corners means that we provide you with the best options possible. Come see us today, we are open seven days a week and are an easy walk from Bart, Bus Stops, a few minutes from the highway, and close to some of the best cycling in the world.
